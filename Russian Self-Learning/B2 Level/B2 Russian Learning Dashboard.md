## Grammatical Foundations (Level B2)

### 1. The Narrative Engine

- **Aspect & Time:** Perfective = completed foreground event. Imperfective = ongoing background or habitual. Gerunds allow compression of two simultaneous or sequential events into one clause.
- **Passive constructions** shift focus from agent to result — dominant in financial and institutional prose.

### 2. Analytical Writing

- **Discourse connectors** and **nominalized genitives** are the two structural signatures of Russian analytical and journalistic writing. Mastering them unlocks reading comprehension of RIA Novosti and financial reports.
