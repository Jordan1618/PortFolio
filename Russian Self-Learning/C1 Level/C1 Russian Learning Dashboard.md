## ## Grammatical Foundations (Level C1)

_Synthesized from professional and strategic goals_

### 1. Strategic Stylistics

- **Linguistic Registers:** The same content can appear in oral familiar, neutral, journalistic, literary, or administrative register — choosing correctly signals full mastery.
- **Chained genitives and nominalisations** are the structural signature of Russian institutional and analytical writing. A C1 speaker produces them fluidly.

### 2. Morphological Mastery

- **Derivation:** Prefixes and suffixes allow reading unknown words analytically — a critical autonomy skill at this level.
- **Archaic and legal forms:** Recognizing and using -ом/-ем passive participles and subjunctive чтобы structures positions you for reading legal texts and classical literature.
