### **Mistakes learned by theme**

#### Prepositions

|Error|Why|General lesson|
|---|---|---|
|apply on vs apply to|literal translation of French "sur"|every English verb has its own fixed preposition, independent of French|
|if vs whether|both translate to "si" in French but have different uses|whether = alternative/choice (often "or not"); if = real condition|

#### Word formation & derivation

|Error|Why|General lesson|
|---|---|---|
|under-OU vs sub-OU|preposition vs prefix confusion|sub- is the standard prefix for hierarchy/category in technical English|
|Administration vs Administrative Templates|wrong derivation guess|check the exact official term, don't guess the derivation|
|complexify|non-standard English verb|use "complicate" instead|
|enforced vs enforcement|adjective/participle instead of the noun|keep the adjective (state) and noun (concept/mechanism) forms separate|

#### Word order

|Error|Why|General lesson|
|---|---|---|
|word order with "only"|copying French word order|"only" goes right before the thing it modifies|
|Values by default vs default values|French word order carried over|in English the adjective/determiner goes before the noun|

#### Phrasal verbs & fixed expressions

|Error|Why|General lesson|
|---|---|---|
|missing particles (logged in, come from)|phrasal verbs have no visible equivalent in French|always check phrasal verbs, the particle is mandatory|
|no matters what|confusion with a conjugated verb|fixed invariable expression, learn it as a chunk|
|by so far|blending two expressions ("so far" and "by far")|"by far" alone means far and away, never combine them|

#### Verb structure & conjugation

|Error|Why|General lesson|
|---|---|---|
|let / leave|confusion between "to permit" and "to leave in a state"|let = permit, leave = leave in a state|
|is plus should together|stacking two question structures|one modal is enough to invert a question|
|infinitive after "to" + adjective vs adverb|verb/noun and adjective/adverb confusion|no -s after "to"; use an adverb (-ly) to modify a verb|

#### Technical vocabulary / false friends

|Error|Why|General lesson|
|---|---|---|
|msi packets vs packages|false friend|packet = network packet, package = software package|
|security/WMI sort vs filter|wrong technical term|check the field's fixed vocabulary instead of translating the general sense|
