# English Self-Learning

---

## Main Topics

This space tracks my English self-study journey: structured courses, vocabulary building, and a running log of mistakes I've made and corrected turning errors into deliberate practice instead of repeating them silently.

---

## Goal of this Documentation

- **Track Progress:** Keep a clear record of what I've covered and where I actually stand, rather than a vague sense of "I'm fine in English."
- **Quick Reference:** A place to look up vocabulary and grammar points instead of re-searching the same word or rule twice.
- **Learn from Mistakes:** Force myself to write down _why_ an error happened, not just the correction this is what actually makes it stick.
